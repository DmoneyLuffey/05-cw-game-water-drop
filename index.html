Catch Clean Water

Move the water can to catch clean drops and avoid contaminated drops.

1. Use Arrow Left and Arrow Right to move the water can.

2. Catch blue drops to gain +10 score and fill water.

3. Avoid brown drops. Catching one costs 1 life and -10 score.

4. Gold super drops are rare power-ups: they give +1 life.

5. Fill to 100% to advance levels. Beat all 10 levels to win.

You start with 3 lives. Blue gives +10, gold gives +1 life, brown is harmful.
